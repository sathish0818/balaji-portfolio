VOICE-NOTE TESTIMONIALS
=======================

Drop your testimonial voice notes in THIS folder, then update the
paths in  js/main.js  (the TESTIMONIALS array).

Supported formats: .mp3  .wav  .m4a
Expected filenames (default):
  voice-1.mp3
  voice-2.mp3
  voice-3.mp3
  voice-4.mp3
  voice-5.mp3
  voice-6.mp3

Until you add files, each card's player will simply show
"add audio file" and disable itself — the rest of the site works fine.
